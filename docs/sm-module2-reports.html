üíæ Saving Filter Combinations

If you frequently use the same filter combination:

  1. Apply your desired filters
  2. Click "Save As" to create a new report variant
  3. Give it a descriptive name (e.g., "My Team - This Month")
  4. Save to your personal reports folder
‚ö†Ô∏è Don't Modify Shared Reports If you want to change filters permanently, save a copy to your personal folder rather than modifying shared reports that others rely on.
Step 4 of 6

Key Metrics to Track

Important KPIs for recruiting success

üìà Essential Recruiting Metrics

Conversion Rates

  • Interview to Hire Rate - % of interviews resulting in hires (target: 20-30%)
  • Phone Screen to Interview - % advancing past initial contact
  • Source Conversion - Which sources produce the best candidates

Volume Metrics

  • New Candidates/Week - Pipeline fill rate
  • Interviews/Week - Team activity level
  • Hires/Month - Ultimate success measure

Efficiency Metrics

  • Time to Hire - Days from first contact to hire date
  • Pipeline Velocity - How quickly candidates move through stages

üéØ Setting Benchmarks

Metric Good Great
Interview to Hire Rate 20% 30%+
Time to Hire 45 days 30 days
Interviews per Week (per recruiter) 5 8+
üí° Trend Analysis Look at metrics over time, not just snapshots. A declining conversion rate is more concerning than a single low month.

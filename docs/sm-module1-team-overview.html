Recruiter Performance

Compare and track individual recruiter metrics

üë• Individual Recruiter Metrics

The Recruiter Performance section shows a breakdown by each team member, allowing you to identify top performers and those who may need support.

üì∏
Screenshot: Recruiter Performance Table

Metrics Shown Per Recruiter:

  • Active Pipeline - Current candidates being worked
  • Interviews Completed - This month vs last month
  • Conversion Rate - % of interviews leading to hires
  • Avg Time to Hire - Days from first contact to hire

üìä Using Performance Data

This data helps you:

  • Identify recruiters who are exceeding targets for recognition
  • Spot recruiters who may need coaching or additional resources
  • Balance workload across your team
  • Set realistic goals based on historical performance
‚ö†Ô∏è Context Matters Numbers alone don't tell the whole story. A recruiter with fewer hires might be focusing on quality or working harder-to-fill positions. Use these metrics as conversation starters, not judgments.
Step 4 of 6

Pipeline By Stage

Visualize candidate distribution across the hiring funnel

üîÑ Pipeline Stage Breakdown

This visualization shows how many candidates are in each stage of the recruiting process across your entire team:

üì∏
Screenshot: Pipeline Stage Funnel/Chart

Pipeline Stages:

  1. New - Recently added, not yet contacted
  2. Phone Screen - Initial outreach/qualification
  3. Interview Scheduled - Formal interview set up
  4. Interview Complete - Awaiting decision
  5. Offer - Offer extended
  6. Hired - Accepted and onboarding

üéØ What to Look For

  • Bottlenecks - Too many candidates stuck at one stage
  • Empty Stages - Gaps in the pipeline that may cause future problems
  • Healthy Funnel - Natural progression with decreasing numbers at each stage
üí° Weekly Review Review the pipeline breakdown in your weekly team meetings. Ask recruiters about candidates who have been in the same stage for more than 2 weeks.

Navigate deeper into specific data

üîç Filtering Options

Use filters to focus on specific subsets of your team's data:

  • By Recruiter - View one recruiter's metrics in isolation
  • By Date Range - Compare different time periods
  • By Source - See which lead sources perform best
  • By Status - Focus on specific pipeline stages

üìã Drill-Down to Records

From any metric or chart, you can click to see the underlying records:

  1. Click a metric card, chart segment, or table row
  2. A filtered list view opens showing matching records
  3. Click any record to view full details
  4. Use browser back button or navigation to return to dashboard
‚ÑπÔ∏è Export Data From list views, you can export data to Excel/CSV for further analysis or to share with leadership.
